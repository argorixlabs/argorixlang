# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include
demonstrating empathy and kindness, being respectful of differing opinions, and
gracefully accepting constructive feedback. Unacceptable behavior includes
harassment, trolling, insulting comments, and publishing others' private
information.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers at **conduct@argorixlabs.dev**. All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

For the full text, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
